How Sorvec handles contact information.

Last updated: 26 June 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to Sorvec Pty Ltd (ABN 41 698 689 075) and explains how we handle information provided through business, project and recruitment enquiries.

2. Information we collect

When you contact Sorvec, we may collect your name, email address, organisation, role, message content, recruitment materials and any other information you choose to provide.

3. How we use information

We use contact information to respond to enquiries, assess potential engagements, manage recruitment correspondence, maintain business records and meet legal or operational obligations.

4. Third-party services

This V6 website is designed to use local CSS and local assets only, reducing third-party page requests. Email, hosting, analytics or security providers may still process limited technical or contact information if those services are used by Sorvec outside this static page.

5. Disclosure and retention

We do not sell contact information. We may disclose information to service providers, professional advisers, regulators or counterparties where reasonably required for business, legal, security or recruitment purposes. We retain information only for as long as it is reasonably needed.

6. Access and contact

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you, subject to applicable law. Contact us at service@sorvec.com.
